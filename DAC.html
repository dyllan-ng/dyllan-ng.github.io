D.A.C Shelter

D.A.C Shelter is a animal shelter that helps dogs and cats
to have food,water and a bed so they live normally and get
adopted by people.

What are we about?

We are about a shelter that brings homeless dogs to
have food and water and a bed so they can be happy instead
of being on the street and causing accidents because
they could walk to a moving car and get hurt and another cars might crash
in to the the first car because of stop immediatly.
